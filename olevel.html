      1. How can a bimetallic strip be made?

        1. Suppose 105 g of water at 75 °C is added to 215 g of cold water at 15 °C and the mixture is well stirred, determine the final temperature of the mixture if the heat absorbed by the container is neglected.

        Solution:

        Step 1: Identify the topic from which the question is coming from, for example in part (a), the question is from the topic called thermal expansion which is taught in form three.

        Step 2:Ater reading the question carefully and understand the need of the question, for example in this question (i) we are required to explain briefly how bimetallic strip is made in (ii) we are asked to show what happens when a bimetallic strip made by brass and iron is heated.

        Step 3: Let answer the question.

        (i) Bimetallic strip is made by joining two different metals that expand diffrently when heated at the same temperature, for example brass and or brass and invar

        (ii)When a bimetallic strip made by both brass and iron is heated, the bimetallic strip bends towards iron making brass forms the outside part while iron forms the inside part of the curve because brass expands faster than iron at the same temperature change as shown in the figure below.

        In part (b) the question is from the topic of measurement of thermal energy which is taught in form three.

        Data given.

        For hot water.

        mass, m1 = 105 g.

        Initial temperature, θ1 75 °C.

        For cold water.

        mass. m2= 215 g.

        Initial temperature, θ2 15 °C.

        Required to find the final temperature of mixture, θ.

        From the principle of conservation of energy

        Heat lost by hot water = Heat gained by cold water.

        Assumption:Heat gained by container is neglected

        Heat = mcΔθ

        m1x c x (θ1-θ) = m2x c x (θ - θ2).

        Where c is the specific heat capacity of water

        105 g x (75 °C - θ) = 215 g x (θ -15 °C)

        θ = 34.69 °C.

        Therefore, the final temperature of the mixture is 34.69 °C.
